Job Title: Data Analyst

Company: TechSolutions Inc.

Location: New York, NY (Hybrid)

About Us: TechSolutions Inc. is a fast-growing technology company dedicated to providing innovative solutions for data management. We are looking for a motivated Data Analyst to join our dynamic team and help us make sense of complex data.

Position Summary: We are seeking a Data Analyst to support our product and marketing teams with insightful data analysis. The ideal candidate will have a knack for turning raw data into actionable insights. You will be responsible for building dashboards, running reports, and analyzing trends to support our business decisions.

Key Responsibilities:

Analyze complex datasets using SQL and Python to identify trends and patterns.

Develop and maintain dashboards and reports using visualization tools like Tableau or Power BI.

Present findings and recommendations to stakeholders in a clear and concise manner.

Collaborate with engineering and product teams to collect new data.

Perform ad-hoc analysis to answer specific business questions.

Ensure data accuracy and integrity in all reports.

Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Economics, Computer Science, or a related field.

1-2 years of experience in a data analysis role.

Strong proficiency in SQL.

Experience with Python (Pandas, NumPy) for data manipulation.

Hands-on experience with a BI tool such as Tableau or Power BI.

Excellent communication and presentation skills.

Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.
